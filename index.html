Who is GoblinGob?

Goblin is an AI research and deployment company that aims to create safe and beneficial artificial general intelligence (AGI) for the benefit of humanity. AGI refers to AI systems that are geny smarter than humans and can outperform humans at most economically valuable work. The company also intends to collaborate with other research organizations and individuals, and to make its research and patents open to the public, except in cases where they could negatively affect safety.
